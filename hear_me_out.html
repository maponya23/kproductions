'Hear Me Out' Youth Expression Program

Through this theatrical production, we provided a platform for young individuals to express their talent on various stages, providing them with much needed exposure. This platform also allows for the expression of societal ills through the medium of creative arts. By fostering self- expression, we encouraged open dialogue and a sense of belonging among participants.
